Major Challenges and Solutions

Challenge 1: Chronic Workforce Shortage

  • 66% of operators rate certified technician hiring as "moderately to very difficult"
  • >50% expect attrition to worsen in 2026
  • 2025 labor cost inflation: 5.5–6.0% (vs. ~3.0% pre-COVID baseline)
  • Solutions: LAUNCH Global Access Program (500+ technicians, 79 countries); GE Aerospace multi-region workforce investment; Oliver Wyman × IAWA "Lift Off To Leadership" study

Challenge 2: Next-Generation Engine Cost Overruns

  • 66% of operators report next-gen narrow-body engine maintenance costs exceeding expectations by >21%
  • 25% report costs exceeding expectations by >50%
  • Context: GTF durability recovery ongoing but not complete; LEAP durability concerns emerging

Challenge 3: Geopolitical Disruption — Tariffs and Conflict

  • 90% of operators experiencing direct tariff impacts
  • Geopolitical instability ranked #2 industry disruptor (new entry)
  • US-Iran conflict impact: Global Aircraft Engine MRO market trajectory fundamentally altered
