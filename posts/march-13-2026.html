Major Challenges and Solutions

Critical Challenges

#### 1. Skilled Labor Shortage (Top Priority)

  • North America: Severe shortage of qualified technicians, engineers, and planners
  • Middle East: Critical gap in technical workforce
  • Retirement Wave: Experienced mechanics retiring faster than new entrants join
  • Skill Gap: Need for expertise in new generation aircraft and composite materials

Solutions:

  • Enhanced partnership with technical schools and training institutions
  • Competitive compensation packages
  • Clear career development pathways
  • Investment in modern training platforms
  • AR/VR-based training programs

#### 2. Rising Costs

  • Labor cost increases outpacing airline revenue growth
  • Material cost escalation
  • Capacity investment requirements
  • Geopolitical factors driving tariff fluctuations

Solutions:

  • Productivity improvements through technology adoption
  • Predictive maintenance reducing unplanned expenses
  • Long-term partnerships to lock in capacity and pricing
  • Efficiency gains through digitalization

#### 3. Supply Chain Instability

  • Extended delivery cycles for specific components
  • Geopolitical risks affecting international trade
  • Parts availability constraints affecting turnaround times

Solutions:

  • Proactive planning and increased inventory buffers
  • Higher component repair rates
  • Enhanced collaboration with suppliers
  • Utilization of USM markets
  • Investment in local supply chain capabilities

#### 4. Integration and Transformation Pressures

  • Merger Integration: Major airline mergers (e.g., Korean Air with Asiana) creating complex facility and team integration challenges
  • Cultural Integration: Combining different operational cultures and processes
  • System Integration: Merging maintenance management and information systems

Solutions:

  • Careful phased integration planning
  • Standardization of processes and procedures
  • Communication and change management programs
  • Technology platform consolidation

Sustainability Challenges

  • Green Maintenance Practices: Adoption required due to environmental pressure and regulations
  • Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF): Compatibility maintenance requirements
  • Waste Reduction: Solvent recovery and material recycling programs
  • Energy Efficiency: Facilities upgrading to reduce environmental impact

Market Structure Challenges

  • Shift from Price Competition to Capacity Reservation: Airlines moving from "shopping around" to long-term agreements reserving entire production lines
  • North American Structural Heavy Maintenance Shortage: Driving work to offshore locations
