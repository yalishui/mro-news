Major Challenges & Solutions

GTF Engine Recall — Extended Recovery Timeline

Pratt & Whitney's PW1000G inspection and shop visit cycle expected to extend through end of decade, keeping global narrowbody MRO capacity under strain. Robotics, additive manufacturing, and expanded MRO network agreements are key mitigation tools.

Engine TAT — #1 Risk in Asia-Pacific

TAT predictability — not just length — is the primary pain point. Airlines cannot reliably plan fleet availability. Solutions: regional supply chain hubs, modular swap strategies, increased USM/PMA acceptance. Chinese airlines increasingly adopting these tools.

Feedstock Price Inflation

AerSale's 1-in-10 bid win rate illustrates competitive intensity for used aircraft/engine feedstock. Some buyers paying prices that industry veterans consider commercially unsustainable long-term — market correction risk growing.

Environmental Compliance Costs

EU REACH regulations forcing MRO shops to transition away from chromium and cadmium plating — significant capital investment required. Ametek MRO's £1M+ environmental upgrade is emblematic of compliance costs across European MROs.

Workforce Shortages

CAAS Singapore fast-tracking MRO workforce training. Thrust Institute of Maintenance opened at Fort Worth Spinks Airport (10-month FAA AMT curriculum). American Airlines donated MD-80 APU to Chicago School of Aviation Maintenance. Ametek MRO deploying AR and AI tools to accelerate technician onboarding.
