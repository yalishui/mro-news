VSE Corporation Closes $2.025B Acquisition of Precision Aviation Group — Creates 61-Site Global Platform

  • Close date: May 5, 2026
  • Seller: GenNx360 Capital Partners
  • Combined platform: 61 locations across 8 countries; 48 repair facilities; 11 distribution centers
  • Revenue impact: Increases VSE revenue by ~50% on a pro forma 2025 basis
  • EBITDA: Immediately accretive; path to exceeding 20% consolidated Adjusted EBITDA margins
  • Funding: February 2026 equity/tangential equity unit offerings + $900M Term Loan B (2033)
  • New debt: led by RBC Capital Markets; legal counsel by Jones Day
  • FY2026 revenue guidance updated: 57%–61% growth (previously 19–23%)
  • FY2026 Adjusted EBITDA margin guidance: 18.1%–18.5% (previously 16.8–17.3%)
  • Net leverage ratio: Improved to 2.0x from 2.3x pre-acquisition
  • CEO John Cuomo: "The addition of PAG meaningfully expands our global footprint, strengthens our repair capabilities, and enhances our ability to deliver integrated, end-to-end solutions to our customers. PAG's margin profile is immediately accretive and supports a clear path to exceeding 20% consolidated Adjusted EBITDA margins over time."
  • NorthStar Technologies also acquired April 1, 2026 — MRO and third-party logistics for B&GA market; included in Q1 results
