1. Ryanair Announces £40 Million MRO Expansion at Prestwick

Date: March 23, 2026

Source: Aviation Week

Ryanair has unveiled ambitious plans to expand its maintenance operations at Prestwick Airport in Scotland:

  • Investment: £40 million ($53.3 million USD)
  • Capacity Increase: Expanding from 6 to 10 maintenance bays
  • New Facilities: Additional component repair shops on-site
  • Job Creation: 450 new engineering and technical positions, including 60 apprenticeships
  • Government Support: £4.9 million from UK government, £15+ million from Scottish Government and Scottish Enterprise

Strategic Significance: This expansion will make Prestwick Ryanair's largest heavy maintenance hangar. The LCC currently operates ~647 aircraft (400+ 737NG, 140+ 737-8-200) with plans to exceed 800 aircraft by 2034. Ryanair performs approximately 75% of its heavy maintenance in-house.
